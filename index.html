OUR STORY





              





Heritage board limited is one of the leading manufacturing companies of pre laminated and plain particle board. With 5 manufacturing plants with avantgarde set up,

Heritage board limited is the only Indian company with in-house production facility across all the plants in major cities of India and equipped to produce the pre-laminated wood base, bagasse base and MDF board to cater diverse demand of furniture industry.
