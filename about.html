ABOUT THE COMPANY

Heritage Board Ltd was incorporated in 2004 under Indian company's act 1956. We are amongst the prestigious names in the industry engaged in production, distributor, wholesaler, retailer and Interior Designing products like MDF, Particle boards, pre lamination, Decorative Surfaces, Laminates etc. Our entire product range & designs are treasured among the clients for its optimum quality and exceptional strength. They are creatively designed as per the preferences of our clients. We offer them in customized and standardized options and delivered them in Committed time frame. These products are functionally advanced in nature and are used in different industrial applications.

We are supported by well-developed and advanced infrastructural facility that is situated in 5 Product units that is spread across the sprawling area. Our facility is divided into different sections that assist us to execute the entire business operations in a systematic manner. Our manufacturing units are outfitted with advanced technologies and tools that assist us to deliver the entire product range in a short period of time.

We are supported by the team of experienced professionals who have strong domain expertise in their respective domain. These professionals ensure that the entire product range is delivered in well-defined time frame. Our team leaders have a vast industrial experience and strong domain expertise which assist us to keep a step ahead among the competitors.
